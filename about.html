About Us

Learn more about how we do all that magic with your credit score!

Who We Are

Active CS is a company that will guide you throughout the way of improving your credit score and will assist you in recovering from damaging information on your credit report. Our team is driven by consumer protection laws (Fair Credit Reporting Act and Fair Debt Collections Practices Act) and is targeted towards the most effective methods of detecting and disputing reporting inaccuracies. Active CS uses modern software that allows us to help a lot of people every day. Working hand in hand with experienced attorneys who are specialized in the field of credit reporting inaccuracies, Active CS will not only give you the chance to boost your credit score and delete all possible mistakes that make you untrustworthy for your potential lenders and employers, but will also make its best to explain how to effectively maintain your credit history.

What We Do

The entire process of work is customer-oriented and is based on the individual needs and issues of each of our clients. The first step of work pertains to a thorough analysis of the clients` Credit Reports to find all of the errors and inaccuracies that were made by Credit Reporting Agencies. Based on the detected mistakes, we draft dispute letters and send them to each CRA. The next step - receiving the updated Credit Reports, will show us what corrections were made and what errors were rectified. At this stage, we compare initial and updated reports and, in case the clients` requests were not considered, we prepare a set of documents and claims that will be filed in the court against Credit Reporting Agencies and other furnishers of damaging information.

This is when our experienced attorneys come to help. They represent our clients in court and conduct lawsuits on their behalf. When they win the case, you will get a portion of statutory damages. The attorneys get paid out of the winnings as well. As you can see, you do not have to suffer any costs, but on the contrary, you will be compensated.

Eventually:

  • Your Credit Report is cleared of any potentially inaccurate information;
  • Your credit score rises;
  • You get more chances for loans and credits;
  • You get remunerated after the successful lawsuit against CRAs.

Your credit reporting mistakes - your ladder to financial freedom!
