How it Works

Learn more about how we do all that magic with your credit score!

SIGN UP

The process is simple. Follow the below steps:

  1. After you register on activecreditscore.com and fill in the sign up form, you will get all the necessary instructions on how to get your free credit reports.
  2. Credit Karma, Privacy Guard, Identity IQ or Smart Credit offer you offer you 3 in 1 credit reports. Choose one of these tri-merge credit reports and upload it in your account.
  3. Your Credit reports will be thoroughly analyzed. Based on your personal needs/credit issues/errors found, three dispute letters will be drafted and sent to the major Credit Reporting Agencies - TransUnion, Equifax and Experian.

Correction stage

  1. You will receive responses from TransUnion, Equifax and Experian within 45 days and will upload them on your personal platform.
  2. There will be 30 more days within which all the CRAs can correct the inaccuracies in your Credit Reports.

Analysis of the work performed

  1. Once you receive the updated Credit Reports, you will upload them in your account.
  2. All new Credit Reports will be examined and reviewed, and if the errors were corrected, your Credit Score will undoubtedly rise.
  3. If the Credit Reporting Agencies failed to correct the information you disputed, a lawsuit will be filed against them. This stage does not require any extra expense from you.
  4. On top of that, after the court`s decision, you are entitled to your monetary compensation.
